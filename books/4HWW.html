One week out

  • Decide on a schedule on a routine batched tasks (e-mail, online banking, ...)
  • Save important documents to a small storage device.
  • Pack a single small backpack.
  • Move in briefly with a family member or friend.

Two days out

  • Put remaining automobiles into storage or a friend's garage.
  • Put fuel stabilizer like Sta-Bil in the gas tanks
  • Disconnect the negative leads from batteries to prevent drain.
  • Put the vehicles on jack stands to prevent tire.
  • Cancel all auto insurance except for the theft coverage.
